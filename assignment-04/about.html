Rupi Kaur is a 26 year old Indian-born canadian who is well known for her poetry books; milk and honey, and the sun and her flowers. She got her degree in Rhetoric Studies, which lead her to be such a great writer. Her 'milk and honey' publication sold over a million copies all while remaining on the new york times best sellers list for over a year.
