Why Acai Bowls?

Acai bowls are amazing. I discovered them only a couple years ago and since then my life has been changed. It helps me keep up on my fruit intake and is an easy substitute for sweets. It takes some time to perfect the ratio for your bowl, but it is possible. My favorite acai bowl in Missoula is Mountain Berry Bowls, it's a food truck the only comes in the summer/spring and this winter has been hard without it!
